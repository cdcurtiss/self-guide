# Standard Operating Procedure: Vendor Load-In

**Effective Date:** September 2016  
**Scope:** All food, retail, and activation vendors for the Outdoor Stage.

## 1. Overview
This document outlines the safety protocols and timeline for vendor arrival at the Pygmalion Festival. Strict adherence to this schedule is required to comply with Champaign-Urbana city fire codes.

> **CRITICAL:** Vehicles must be cleared from the midway 60 minutes prior to gates opening. No exceptions.

## 2. Load-In Schedule

Vendors are assigned a wave based on their power requirements and location.

| Wave | Time | Vendor Type | Zone |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **A** | 08:00 AM | Food Trucks (Heavy/Generator) | North Lot |
| **B** | 09:30 AM | Retail Tents (10x10) | Main Midway |
| **C** | 10:45 AM | Sponsors & Activation | Entry Gate |

## 3. Vehicle Access Protocol

Drivers must follow these steps to enter the event perimeter:

1. Enter via the **West Checkpoint** on Walnut St.
2. Present your *Vendor Permit* (sent via email) to the Site Ops Manager.
3. Activate hazard lights and maintain a speed of **< 5 MPH**.
4. Unload equipment immediately at your marked location.
5. Move vehicle to the designated off-site parking lot.
